# Dlytful Product Brief

## The One-Liner

**Dlytful translates what you built into what people see.**

---

## The Problem

Indie developers ship functional apps with Bolt, Cursor, and Replit. The code works. But when someone lands on their site, they see a generic template. Same fonts as 10,000 other startups. No clear message. No reason to remember them.

Branding is usually an afterthought. Most founders can't describe what "looks right" until they see it. They can't write the prompt that would make their app feel like *theirs*.

---

## The Solution

Dlytful is a guided discovery flow that extracts what founders *meant* to build and translates it into prompts they can paste directly into Bolt. The output transforms their existing app's UI, typography, colors, and voice without touching functionality.

The core experience: answer a few questions, get a prompt, paste it, watch your app transform. The "aha moment" is the product.

---

## The Name

"Dlytful" captures the essence of a perfect summer day. That rare, golden moment where everything aligns effortlessly. Warm but not harsh. Bright but not glaring. The day your friends bring up months later.

This is the energy Dlytful helps indie developers create in their products.

Your existing domain dlytful.com reflects this perfectly and works across all contexts.

Solo founders, indie hackers, and small teams who ship products with intention. People who'd rather have 100 users who truly get it than 1,000 who merely use it.

They've built something real. They just need help making it look like it has a soul.

## Core Outputs

### The Rebrand Prompt

Designed for existing apps. Keeps all functionality, transforms the UI and voice.

Users paste this into Bolt with their existing project. The prompt includes visual identity, voice direction, and component-level guidance. Nothing breaks. Everything transforms.

### The Foundation Prompt

For users who haven't started building yet. Full brand direction from the start.

### The Discoverability Prompt (dlytful max)

Structured metadata that users paste into Bolt to inject into their app's HTML. Includes Open Graph tags, Schema.org structured data, and custom meta attributes optimized for how products get discovered and recommended.

One paste. Deployed. Discoverable.

### The Agent System Prompt (dlytful max)

For products with conversational UI. Captures the brand's voice so the product's AI component sounds like the brand.

## The Discovery Flow

Based on the Dlytful Discovery Conversation framework. Five parts, each unlocking deeper outputs:

**Part 1: The Product in Practice.** Walk me through the moment someone uses this and thinks "this is good."

**Part 2: The Person Who Gets It.** Who immediately understands what you've built?

**Part 3: What You're Not.** What do you absolutely not want your brand to feel like?

**Part 4: The Moment.** Describe the feeling of a perfect day. The energy you want your product to create.

**Part 5: Validation.** Here's what I'm hearing. Does this feel right?

---

## Archetype Selection

Users see a wheel of 12 brand archetypes. Based on their discovery answers, 2-3 archetypes are highlighted with warm color. Others are desaturated.

The system explains why those archetypes align with their inputs. Users can override if it doesn't feel right.

**dlytful zero:** Result shown, no selector.

**dlytful one:** 6 archetypes available, can select primary + secondary.

**dlytful max:** Full 12, with recommended combo highlighted and explained.

## Key UX Principles

**Fork and iterate.** Sprints can be forked from any point. Iteration is core to the founder journey.

**No "AI" mentions.** Everyone knows. Keep it under the hood. The technology is invisible. The outcome is everything.

**Clean exports.** Markdown and Notion templates feel like theirs, not Dlytful's.

**The aha moment is the product.** Everything else is scaffolding.

## What Dlytful Is Not

Dlytful is not a logo generator. Not a template library. Not a design tool.

Dlytful is a translation layer. It extracts what founders meant and converts it into language that Bolt understands.

Great products already have a soul. Dlytful helps them speak.
